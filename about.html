Origin Story

Why I Built This Agency

After years working as a technical consultant across industries — from SaaS startups to established service businesses — I kept noticing a pattern. The businesses that were struggling weren't short of work ethic or ambition. They were bleeding revenue from the same four or five fixable leaks: no lead follow-up system, manual work that should be automated, poor visibility on Google, and funnels that looked right but were broken underneath.

The advice they were getting from marketing agencies was generic, the tools they were sold were too complex to use, and the results — when they came at all — were impossible to measure. I built VantageScale to be the antidote to that: a diagnostic-first consultancy that speaks in outcomes, not outputs, and builds exactly what your business needs to stop losing money.

Every engagement starts with a diagnosis. Every recommendation ties to a business outcome. Every system we build is tracked so you can see exactly what it's doing for your revenue. That's the only way I know how to work.
