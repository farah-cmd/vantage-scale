Common Questions About SEO / AEO / GEO

What is AEO and why does it matter?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) is the practice of optimising your content to appear in AI-powered search results and featured snippets — Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, ChatGPT search, and Bing Copilot. As more searches end without a click, appearing as the answer is more valuable than appearing on page one.

How long does it take to see results?

Technical SEO improvements can show ranking movement within 4–8 weeks. Competitive keyword rankings typically take 3–6 months. AEO/GEO visibility in AI tools can appear faster — often within 6–10 weeks of structured content being indexed.

Do I need to be technical to use this?

No. You need to know your business and your customers. We handle all the technical implementation, content strategy, and structured data — and report in plain English.
