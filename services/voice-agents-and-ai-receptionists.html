Quick Answer

What does an AI receptionist do?

An AI receptionist answers inbound phone calls using a natural-sounding voice, gathers caller information, answers common questions, qualifies leads, and books appointments directly into your calendar — all without any human involvement.

Here's What's Actually Happening in Your Business Right Now

Calls come in during meetings, after hours, or when your team is fully occupied — and those calls go to voicemail. Most callers don't leave a message. They call the next business on the list.

You have no reliable way to know how many calls you're missing — your phone system only tracks connected calls, not the ones that rang out or were abandoned.

A receptionist handles calls during business hours, but evenings, weekends, and holidays are uncovered — which is exactly when many people decide they need your service and pick up the phone.

Front-desk staff spend a significant portion of each day answering the same routine questions — pulling their attention away from in-person customers standing right in front of them.
